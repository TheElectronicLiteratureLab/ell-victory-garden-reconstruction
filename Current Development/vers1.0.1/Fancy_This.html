Fancy This

Billy Van crossed meaty arms over his chest and slouched against the nose of the Rainbow Bus. "Howdy there, Little Runbird. I did not know that you and the Professor were, might I say, closely acquainted." Putting on a deeply regrettable leer.

"Go lick an armadillo," Veronica told him.

Billy Van mustered up a wink to go with the leer. "No offense I'm sure." He resettled the Presidente atop his great squarish head. Thea just stared, beginning to notice how unreality had escaped the TV box.

"It's a surprise," Billy Van said to Veronica. "Did not expect to cross trails with you tonight. But now that's happened, I wonder could you could answer me a little question."
