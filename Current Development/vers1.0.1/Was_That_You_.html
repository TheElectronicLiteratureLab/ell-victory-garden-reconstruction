Was That You?

"Eat cactus and die," Veronica suggested.

Billy Van inspected his left bootheel. "You see, Ms. Runbird," he related, "I was over to the Just Say No a little earlier in the evening, and found the place in quite a state. Folks related some real strange shit happening just before I came on scene."

"It's that sort of town," Veronica reminded him. "Keep Tara terrific."

"What I did hear," Billy Van continued, "was that some young lady very much like yourself went, I believe the term is loco, up in the place. Word was she slapped a few folks around and smashed the bar up pretty good."

Thea looked at Veronica and blinked. "Say what?"

"Would that've happened to be you?" Billy Van wondered.
