Statement

"Thanks kindly, stranger," Billy Van said, letting out the slippery clutch. "We'd offer you a ride but there's a bunch of us up in here." He paused a few feet further on. "And we don't give rides to peckerwood fairies ..."

Somebody tossed a beer bottle in Leroy's general direction and the Disaster Bus walrused into an abrupt turn. Leroy sprinted after, feeling a spreading numbness in the pit of his stomach.

"Come on," he heard Billy Van Saxgutter say to his confederates, "we got a statement to make."

The bus rattled off with a sharp backfire and belch of burnt oil.
