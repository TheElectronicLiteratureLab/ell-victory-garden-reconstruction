Joker

Things might not have gone much further had tonight's activity been entirely a Sigma Omega fraternity joint. But as previously noted there was a joker in the deck, or to be accurate, at the wheel of the Rainbow Riders tour bus, who after driving around the block a few times in confusion, finally managed to back onto Thea Agnew's front lawn. The marchers pulled up short and collected quickly around this new point of interest.

"Unholy shit," Veronica said when she saw who was extricating himself from the driver's seat.

"Evening all," Billy Van Saxgutter announced, squaring up his string tie and doffing the Stetson. "Does one of you ladies happen to be Professor Dorothea Agnew?"

"Billy Van," Veronica called out to him. "Be cool for shit's sake."

The jovial prankster squinted in her direction. "Is it that little Runnin' Bird, there?"
