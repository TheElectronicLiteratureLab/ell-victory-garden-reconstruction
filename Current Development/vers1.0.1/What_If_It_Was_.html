What If It Was?

Veronica stared up the evil clown's left nostril. "And what's it to you?"

Billy Van scratched a floppy earlobe, pondering implications. "Look see, Runbird, I always did regard you as having more cojones than half the so-called bad dudes in this town."

"I'm touched," Veronica said. "But the SWAT team's overdue. Make your point if you have one."

The mayhematician pulled himself up and straightened his single-starred belt buckle. "Doctor Agnew," he told Thea, "the fellas here and I don't particularly approve of your trying to stomp on Western Civilization and our American way of doing. And I allow as we might have showed here tonight to make a show of that displeasure. But owing to respect for our young friend over there, I am gonna advise my associates to act like they were gentlemen."

Thea snorted. Veronica kept on staring lasers.
