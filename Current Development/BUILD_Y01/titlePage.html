This version is work in progress. The reader interface is not complete. (And there may be holes in the road.) You may move through this version in any of three ways: if there are visible links, you may follow them as on any webpage; you may press the ENTER key at any point to advance along a Path -- an idiosyncratic, hopscotch traversal of the work; or you may press the CONTROL key to advance within a Stream, a more coherent narrative episode. (If you are not in a Stream, or at the end of one, you will see a list of Streams to choose from.)
