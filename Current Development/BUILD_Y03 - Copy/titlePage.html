This version is work in progress. The reader interface is not finished; there may be holes in the road. You can move through this version in any of three ways: if there are visible links, follow them as on any webpage; you may press the UP arrow key at any point to advance along a Path -- an idiosyncratic, hopscotch traversal of the work; press the DOWN ARROW to reverse through the Path. There are also Streams, which are more coherent narrative episodes. Use the RIGHT arrow to move ahead in a Stream, LEFT to move backward. Not all pages are in Streams. Pressing LEFT or RIGHT in this case will take you to a list of Streams to enter.

There is also a graphical map
