Regrets

They'll have shotguns up there, Harley reflected, and they just might shoot out the windows when we get in range. He thought of crawling into the back seat but he'd covered enough highway disasters to know that backseat passengers usually died horribly in the fire. Oh well.

It occurred to him that there were two things he deeply regretted. First, that he'd never make love with Veronica again, and second, that he wouldn't get to beat the living crap out of Urquhart for it.
