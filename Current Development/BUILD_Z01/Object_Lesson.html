Thea decided to add a little input. "What's that to you, pilgrim?"

Billy Van turned a malevolent eye her way. "Ah was comin' to that," he said, drawing up to his full height and straightening his Lone Star belt buckle. "Ah jus' thought that things bein' what they are, y'all might appreciate a little object lesson in, uh... political expression ."

He reached a long arm into the microbus and hauled back a large canvas sports bag, out of which came a pile of bricks and a half dozen metal baseball bats.

"Now," he continued, kicking the fender of the Rainbow Bus, "this here politically correct hippie faggot vee-hicle, we'll just call this ourobject ." He picked up a brick and flung it at a window, scoring a direct hit on a large peace-symbol decal. "And that," Billy Van explained, "can be our first lesson ."
