This is a Recording

Urquhart pressed Play and held the little unit up to his ear. He heard a blip and then the characteristic fuzz of a phone call picked up by a contact mike.

"Dr. Agnew?"

Silence at first.

"Excuse me, have I reached Dr. Dorothea Agnew?"

"Maybe. What do you want?"

"Dr. Agnew, I represent the Holistic Health Services Consultancy, a national clearinghouse on health-related issues."

"That's nice. What is it you want?"

"We understand you may have reason to need information about exposure to the Human Immunodeficiency Virus."

A click, then another blip as the tape restarts. Thea's voice.

"3:57 a.m., January 10, 1991."
