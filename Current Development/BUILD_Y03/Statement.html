Statement

"Thanks kindly, stranger," Billy Van said, letting out the slippery clutch. "We'd offer you a ride but they's a bunch of us in here as 'tis." He paused a few feet further on. "An' anyways we don't give rides to no peckerwood faggots ..."

Somebody tossed a beer bottle in Leroy's general direction and the DisasterBus wallowed into an abrupt turn. Leroy sprinted after, feeling a spreading numbness in the pit of his stomach.

"C'mon," he heard Billy Van Saxgutter say to his confederates, "we got us a statement to make."

The bus rattled off with a sharp backfire and a belch of burnt oil.
