Use of Work by Other Artists

Lyrics from two songs are used by permission: "White Subway" by John McDaid and "Wide Open" by Mojo Nixon. The poems in the stream From a Hot Place are by W. Joe Hoppe.

Most of the illustrations and all portrait and cover graphics were produced with DAZ Studio 4 by Digital Art Zone (DAZ). Characters are original designs based on the DAZ Genesis 3 and 8 figures, with other character morphs and materials used incidentally (see below). Many of the images were processed in Adobe Photoshop using an action from the Nice Niche Inks package by Antje, available at Renderosity. In addition to DAZ and Renderosity, I have acquired assets from RenderHub, Turbosquid, and Most Digital Creations. All assets are used non-commercially in an "editorial" context.
