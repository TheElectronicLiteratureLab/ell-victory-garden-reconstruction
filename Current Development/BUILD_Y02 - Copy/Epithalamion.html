Epithalamion

Writing what? I wondered. When I'd known Harley Morgan he'd been strictly a creature of the airwaves, helpless without his camera crew.

Writing I don't really know what, Harley told me. Whatever comes into my head when I sit down at the Mac. Stories, essays, letters to Bernie Shaw.

He wrote a Wedding Ode for Ted and Jane, Veronica boasted. Boy did that suck.

Did not.

Did too, I'm sure, Thea tossed in.
