Watchman

"Eighteen thousand years I been up here," the watchman begins. "Since the second Eisenhower administration. I was put here to watch. What am I supposed to watch for? I thought to ask. That's your lookout, they said, just you watch. So I sat up here and I watched. And I watched. And I watched some more."

"Then you saw it," you suggest.

"No actually I saw it the first five minutes I was up here. But after all I was told to watch, right? so after I saw it I went on watching."

"Well obviously."

"But guess what I saw."
