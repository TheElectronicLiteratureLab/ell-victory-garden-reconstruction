And Now

She's with me now, sleeping over every night until the draft is done. In two days she's off to London for a conference. I'll miss her intensely.

I think we're into a good thing. We share easily, we know what we're trying to accomplish, we seem to work better together than we do on our own. We have a lot of laughs. Maybe this thing goes further, maybe it all blows apart somehow. We don't talk about it; Thea says we're too old for that kind of anxiety, though I'm not quite her age. I'd call it enchantment, myself, but then I'm the sentimental one.

Jealousy is clearly out of place here, but I do have my moments. When that dream of hers comes back, for instance. When Emily's been hovering about our bed.
