Bildung

"Let me get this right," Leroy interpreted, "you thought you could teach your way to a better country."

Thea thought it over. "Yeah," she said. "Laugh if you will, but that's essentially it."

"Gee, Mom. Do you still believe that?"

Thea closed her eyes. It was almost a full minute before she said anything, and for a while Leroy thought she might have lapsed into sleep.

"Yes I do," she said finally. "I do still believe it. How about you?"
