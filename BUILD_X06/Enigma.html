Enigma

Like any maze or enigma, Victory Garden will often bring you back to a place you've already been. Sometimes these familiar moments will be followed by something new. At other times you may find yourself in a loop, in which case you may want to invoke a yield-word. If you hold down the Option and Command (ï£¿) keys, the words that yield will be identified.

You can also call up a list of all possible links leading out of a given moment by clicking on the Browse icon (the open book) on the bar below.

To end your reading, choose the File menu command.
