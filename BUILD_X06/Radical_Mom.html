Radical Mom

"Were you ever arrested?" Leroy wanted to know.

"Jesus what is this, an FBI interview? No I was never arrested, not once, not even on Moratorium Day when Nixon popped half of Washington, D.C. without due process. Commit that one to memory: unconstitutional arrests; massive violations of civil rights."

Leroy made obedient scribbling motions. "Yes ma'am, got it, hewas a crook after all. But what about you, you're telling me you never did anything really outrageous?"

Thea eyed her son narrowly. "Why do I suspect your father's hand in this little inquisition?"

The boy did his best to look innocent. "I was just curious about why you got thrown out of college."
