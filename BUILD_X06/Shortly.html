Shortly

"Now Dexter," the Sarge cautioned, "don't please don't start in with the paranoia, 'cause I am in no kind of goddam mood for it tonight."

Dexter shrugged and flipped his hood down. He was the only one in the platoon who could actually sleep in a CBW suit. But then again, Dexter was one of those lucky guys who could sneak a nap into just about any five minute interval. The kind best advised never to work as long-distance busdrivers.

"Home," Emily was saying. "Home home home."

"Soon," the Whizzer added. "Real real soon."

Dexter threw back his hood, snapped up out of sleep. "Shut the fuck up!" he yelled. "Don't you people know anything about short time?"
