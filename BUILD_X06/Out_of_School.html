Out of School

"They really threw you out, huh?"

"On my ass, and irrevocably. Folks were getting expelled right and left those days and most of them got to come back after a year or two on the straight. But somehow the College couldn't manage to forgive me. Young ladies weren't supposed to be quick with their fists."

"Gee, Mom. Did you ever beat up on Dad?"

She gave him the narrowest of glares.

"Joshing, really." He put up his hands and mimed some counterpunches. "So after Bryn Mawr you spent a few years hanging out, right?"
