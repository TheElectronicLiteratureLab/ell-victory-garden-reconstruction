Stone in Love

Urquhart served out the beer with Germanic precision, mindful of the shape and dynamics of the head. He was preparing to interrogate. "So. What's between you two is something... important?"

Harley nodded vaguely, his mind and eye elsewhere.

"It's what we might call a serious attachment?"

"It's what we might call 'stone in love.'"

Urquhart sipped his beer, considering. The Nice Girls followed into a critique d'amour in which the speaker kept wishing for a Rational Heart.

"Veronica was born on the morning Bobby Kennedy died," he noted. "By which time, I believe, you had technically lost your virginity."
