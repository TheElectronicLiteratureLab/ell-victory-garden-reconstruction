Questions

Madden paused a while, searching out the right words. "Hey Snyder, tell me something. Do you feel okay about this war?"

Snyder shrugged. "Ask me again when they start calling back the fifty-year-old vets."

The sports came on and the bar fell into a kind of disbelieving silence while the local analyst explained how the University of Tara could still be taken seriously after losing 46-3 in the Citronella Bowl.

Snyder cycled back. "Why shouldn't I feel okay about this war?"
