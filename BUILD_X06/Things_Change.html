Proprioceptive incoherence: often among the earliest warning signs of Burroughs' Irrealis, or as we called it in the days before grant money, the Slipping Sickness.

Is this what they mean bywhite-out , you're wondering, when a voice reaches you saying: Pssst, hey meester, over here!

— Ha ha, you say, that's a nice one: where's here? Sounds fading up now of a Really Big lever being swung over and this is where the zones change over with the thunderous snick of a cosmic knifeswitch.
