So Stated

U considered. "A statement?" he ventured.

Madden reached his right hand into his coat. Holy shit, U said to himself and hit the deck hard. Madden came out with his ballpoint and Papermate. Click. "Statement, yeah?"

U blinked back at him from behind the cover of an old PC.

"Go ahead," said Madden. "You should pardon the expression, but shoot."

"Peace now," U told him. "Liberty and justice for all."

Madden stowed the pen and paper, shrugging. "It's kind of a tall order, but we'll do our best." He turned and walked to the door.
