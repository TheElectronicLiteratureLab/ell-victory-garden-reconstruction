While You Can

Veronica kicked off and backstroked over to the woman who'd just spoken. "What did you mean by that?" she asked, treading water.

"Guess you didn't see the paper this morning," the woman told her. "This big developer bought 75 acres just upstream of here. Wants to put in a golf course and luxury condos. Read all about it," she advised, "then get real mad." She turned and headed for the shallows.

Miles paddled to the side and caught his breath. "Oh well," he reflected, "at least this proves I'm not hallucinating. We're still in America as we know it."
