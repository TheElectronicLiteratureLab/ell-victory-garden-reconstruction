Moving Violation

Harley was thinking about just going ahead and strangling the sonofabitch when he noticed a flicker of light reflecting off the dashboard. Red, white, blue. Kind of patriotic.

Harley took a look over his shoulder and his eyes went wide. "Oh perfect."

"It's that sedan isn't it?" Urquhart said. "I don't want to look."

Harley threw a hard right against Urquhart's forearm. Which wasn't such a good idea, since it caused the car to lunge hard to the left, scrabbling and skidding along the shoulder.

"No, you crazy shithead, it's not any damned sedan. It's a fucking police cruiser, and I think you better pull over right now."
