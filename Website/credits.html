Credits

The work to reconstruct Stuart Moulthrop’s Victory Garden was led by the author with support from the Electronic Literature Lab at Washington State University Vancouver. Arlo Ptolemy served as Project Manager and handled the usability testing. Andrew Thompson handled the UI development and assisted Moulthrop with the code. Holly Slocum designed the interface. The archival website was designed by Austin Gohl and coded by Thompson and Gohl. Copywriting was handled by Moulthrop and Dene Grigar. The gif for the website was produced by Sierra O’Neal, and tech support was provided by Greg Philbrook.

Reconstruction Team

About the Project

Victory Garden 2022 is the new web edition of the classic hypertext novel by Stuart Moulthrop published originally by Eastgate Systems, Inc. on 3.5-inch floppy disk in 1991 and CD-ROM in 2002. It was produced by the author with support from the Electronic Literature Lab at Washington State University Vancouver.
