Splashdown

"Folks bathe together 'round these parts."

"Don't be a stranger."

"Come on guy, get wet!"

So what were you going to do, let all these nice people down? Macarthur spread his arms and offered himself to nature. He hit the water more or less broadside, making a very big splash. A general cheer went up from the crowd as Miles came up blowing and snorting. The water did indeed feel wonderful. It even tasted good.

"What do you think?" Thea asked.

"It's amazing," Miles testified.

"That's nice," a woman in a black tank suit called over, "enjoy it while you can."
