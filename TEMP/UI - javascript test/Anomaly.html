Anomaly

Strictly speaking the Creek consisted of a quarter mile of astonishingly pure spring water flowing through a limestone basin of remarkable beauty.

It was one of Creation's great swimming holes, set in the rolling riverside hills south of the city, encircled by live oaks and prairie grass, home to grackles, warblers, bullfrogs and occasional deer.
