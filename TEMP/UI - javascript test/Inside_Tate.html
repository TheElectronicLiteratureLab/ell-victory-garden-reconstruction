Looking up at Tate in the cage now, Madden tried to keep that old memory from coloring his present impressions. Tate did seem just as abstracted, just as high-in-the-sky as that old man back in Wisconsin. But it would be a mistake, he reminded himself, to think that Tate's feet were anywhere but firmly on the ground.
