Was That You?

"Eat cactus and die," Veronica suggested.

Billy Van inspected his left bootheel. "You see, Little Runbird," he related, "Ah was over't the Just Say No a little earlier this evenin', and Ah found the place in quite a state. Folks tole me some real strange shit happened jes' before I got there."

"It's a wild town," Veronica reminded him.

"What Ah heard," Billy Van continued, "was that some filly lookin' very much like yourself went loco in there tonight. Just a little ol' gal but she slapped some folks around an' smashed th' ol' place up purty good."

Thea looked at Veronica and blinked. "Say what?"

"Would that've happen'd t'be you?" Billy Van wondered.
