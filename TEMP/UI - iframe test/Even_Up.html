Even Up

"Is that what this is about?" Madden wondered, working at his scotch. "They didn't get the plan down back in those old jungle days, so now we get a second chance?"

Snyder smiled. "Well ain't that just the American way these days?" Then he turned on him a look that went back over darker ground. "But you know what we're talking about. You were there."

Madden thought about it. "I guess I was. But what kind of fool would ever want to go back?"
