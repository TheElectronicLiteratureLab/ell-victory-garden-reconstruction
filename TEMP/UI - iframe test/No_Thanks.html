No Thanks

Madden dragged a book off the shelf and idly wiped some dust from the binding with his fingertips.

"Thanks but no thanks, Dr. Heidel. I don't think the District Director could get behind that one. The Bureau got enough of a bad aroma from that sort of play back in the fifties. Even Mister Hoover got his bellyful of witchhunting at the end."

Heidel gave him that he-doesn't-understand-me look that Madden knew oh so well.

"You don't get it, do you?"
