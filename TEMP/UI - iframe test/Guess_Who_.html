Guess Who?

"Howdy," Urquhart greeted them, a little nonplussed by their sudden appearance. "What are you all doing in Leisure Suit Larry's secret elevator?"

"Holding an orgy," Harley told him.

"Heisting classified files," Thea confessed.

"Checking out the view from Thirteen," Veronica suggested.

"Uh, just leaving," Macarthur tried.

Urquhart crossed his arms and shrugged. "Well I'm glad I bumped into you anyway. Could you try to remember to get a bottle or two of ouzo for later? I just noticed I'm fresh out."

Each of them nodded at least once. "Sure thing, Boris."
