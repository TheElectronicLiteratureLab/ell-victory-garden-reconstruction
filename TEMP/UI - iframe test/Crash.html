Your speed now exceeds rated limits of any and all available braking surfaces.

You are going to crash.

The rostrum looms up at you, closer & closer & bigger & bigger. The figure on the rostrum stands immobile, his arms still raised. Now he's not above you anymore but somehow he stands in the plane of your trajectory, straight in your way.

You are going to crash into him.

You try backpedaling, as if that would do any good ha ha, but your feet are no longer in contact with anything you could call terra firma. Closer & closer, faster & faster, the walls and faces around you a bleeding blur.
