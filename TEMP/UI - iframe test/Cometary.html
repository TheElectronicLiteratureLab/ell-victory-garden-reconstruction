Cometary

"Probability of what?" Madden asked him.

Tate drew a series of parabolic curves on the whiteboard, their legs bisecting the earth's orbital circle at various points. Madden looked at them and something in his old school memories clicked.

"Comets?" he said. "You're working on the probability of a comet passing close to the earth. What, have you got some dinosaur theory or something?"

Tate considered his drawing critically as if it still lacked something. "Not comets," he explained. "Not dinosaurs. Something much more interesting."

"Like what?"

Tate gestured toward the eyepiece of the telescope, inviting him to look.
