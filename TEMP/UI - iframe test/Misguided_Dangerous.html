Misguided & Dangerous

"Oh." Leroy started to pull off his Nikes but decided he'd wait till his mom had another cigarette. "Mainly what we talked about was you," he told her.

She finished her bourbon and thought about another but felt reluctant somehow. "I'm touched. What could your dear old dad possibly have to say about me?"

"He says you're getting famous because of the Western Civ thing." Famous, was it? "He also says he thinks you're misguided and dangerous."

"Good old Frank."

"He says you always were a closet Maoist."

Time was, Thea reflected, when that little description had been a term of endearment. But that was another century.
