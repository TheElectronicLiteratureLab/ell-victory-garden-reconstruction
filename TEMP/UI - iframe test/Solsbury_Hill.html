Solsbury Hill

^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Presently a large bright blue angel with civilian markings appears out of the terminator and comes swooping over at a speed just shy of escape velocity. The angel barrels in, making a noise like a calliope in difficulties. Its creased neon eyes swivel, scrutinizing the greasy dome of the watchman. It circles around a few times. You discern its nostrils in contraction. It circles a few more times.

You see the surgical steel claws lock into position.

"Oboyoboyoboy," says the watchman, trying not to fidget.

The angel zooms up and snatches you off the tower.
