One of Our Aircraft

"Holy Jesus," Dexter blurted. "What was that, a fuckin' B-52?"

Sergeant Yvonne shook her head. "One of those mothers can take the fillings right out your teeth." She knew this well, having spent some time on the island of Diego Garcia.

"Big transport, probably," the Whizzer thought. "C-5. Maybe some of those weekend warriors forgot the approach pattern and had to do a go-around. Must've thought they were still flying for Air Useless."

"One of ours, anyway," Emily said.

"Well yeah," the Whizzer replied. "That much seems obvious."

There was another silence while everybody waited for someone else to say the unspeakable.
