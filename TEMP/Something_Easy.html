Something Easy

"I think it was around three in the morning we gave up and just lay there with our arms around each other. I fell asleep for a little bit and when I woke up I think it was because Victor was snoring."

"Don't you just hate when that happens."

Emily bit her lip and laughed. "But just laying there felt so good. There was something easy about him, even something familiar, though I remember looking at his face in the moonlight and being surprised that it wasn't Boris."

"Girl, girl."
