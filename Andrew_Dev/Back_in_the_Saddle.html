Back in the Saddle

"Here," she said, handing him her microcassette recorder. "Get some nice sound bites."

He hugged her, feeling her long, slim arms on his shoulders, unaccountably strong. "I don't have to go."

"Of course you do, Harley." She gave him a long kiss and then backed away. "If you don't you'll just sit around the house and watch the tube, and that drives me nuts. Go out and get some air."

"Okay, mom."

"Though I know you, what you really want is airtime." She tried to tweak his rear but he was already through the door, pulling on his walking coat, fishing out his press card, checking to see if the recorder had batteries. Back in the saddle again.
