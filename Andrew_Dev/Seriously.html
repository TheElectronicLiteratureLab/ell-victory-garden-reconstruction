Seriously

"Shakespeare was wrong. The lawyers can wait. First we'll kill all the tenured faculty."

Jude leaned over and punched Gardner semi-affectionately in the chops. "I promise we'll take you seriously some day, Victor."

"Yeah," another colleague chimed in, "like the day you tell them they can keep their grant money, and you don't want that associate professorship either, thanks."

Gardner buried his head in his arms as if to weep. But he remembered to hold up his empty glass in case the waitress happened by.
